Remove or put textures
-----------------------

The textures affects the following parts of the theme:

1.- Titlebars
2.- Toolbars
3.- Menubars


the folder '../Uncomplicated/textures/' contains two subfolders:

'../Uncomplicated/textures/noise/'				(with textures)
'../Uncomplicated/textures/transparent/'		(without textures)

In each of these folders are two files
'background-noise-toolbar.png' and 'metacity-noise.png'

To remove textures:
--------------------------
copy the contents of '../Uncomplicated/textures/transparent/' as follows

copy 'background-noise-toolbar.png' into '../Uncomplicated/gtk-3.0/assets/"
copy 'background-noise-toolbar.png' into '../Uncomplicated/gtk-2.0/assets/"
copy 'metacity-noise.png' into '../Uncomplicated/metacity-1/"

To make textures:
--------------------------
copy the contents of '../Uncomplicated/textures/noise/' as follows

copy 'background-noise-toolbar.png' into '../Uncomplicated/gtk-3.0/assets/"
copy 'background-noise-toolbar.png' into '../Uncomplicated/gtk-2.0/assets/"
copy 'metacity-noise.png' into '../Uncomplicated/metacity-1/"
